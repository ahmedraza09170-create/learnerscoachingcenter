Salman Saeed

MA In English Literature

I am Salman Saeed, an MA in English Literature with 18 years of experience in the field of education. I am currently working as a Section Head in Army Public School and College Hyderabad Cantt, one of the leading educational institutions in the country. I am passionate about teaching and learning, and I strive to create a positive and stimulating learning environment for my students. I have expertise in curriculum development, assessment, and teacher training. I am also interested in research and innovation in education, and I have participated in several national conferences and workshops on various topics related to education.
